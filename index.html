Welcome to My Website

Where we talk about fast food fries

Welcome to our site, where we explore the differences in fast food fries across major restaurants in Australia. The main four we focus on are KFC, McDonald's, Hungry Jack's, and Red Rooster, each offering their own take on fries with varying seasonings, cooking methods, and unique flavors.

KFC

KFC's fries are made from freshly cut potatoes and fried until crispy. They are seasoned with a unique blend of salt and herbs, giving them a distinct flavor that pairs well with KFC's signature fried chicken.

McDonald's

McDonald's fries are prepared from specially selected potatoes and fried in a vegetable oil blend. They are lightly salted and, in some regions, fried in beef tallow for a richer flavor.

Hungry Jack's

Hungry Jack's fries are thick-cut and fried in a blend of vegetable oils, then seasoned with a generous amount of salt..

Red Rooster

Red Rooster fries are made from high-quality potatoes, fried to a crispy finish, and seasoned with a mix of classic salt and a special chicken seasoning.
